The tobacco plays with pork-belly glaze and the firm tannin cuts bitter-brussels char. Strong Super Tuscan for the belly build — Maremma structure meets sweet-savory bite.
- **Porterhouse** — Super Tuscan — Le Pupille Saffredi from the Maremma coast, Cab-Merlot-Petit Verdot, 2021, blackcurrant, tobacco, graphite, Mediterranean herbs, firm-tannin finish. The firm tannin handles dual-texture strip-and-tender and the tobacco plays with the mixed profile. Peak Super Tuscan for Porterhouse — Maremma structure bridges both cuts.
- **Kansas City** — Super Tuscan — Le Pupille Saffredi from the Maremma coast, Cab-Merlot-Petit Verdot, 2021, blackcurrant, tobacco, graphite, Mediterranean herbs, firm-tannin finish. The firm tannin handles lean-bold strip beef and the graphite plays with firm-savory grain. Peak Super Tuscan for KC — Bordeaux-style intensity on the well-marbled cut.
