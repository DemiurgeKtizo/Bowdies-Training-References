# 500-Note Sample Audit — 2026-05-06

**Sample:** 500 stratified picks (60 gold, 80 excellent, 110 strong, 150 works, 100 avoid), deterministic seed 20260506.
**Read in full.** Findings below.

## Tier A — systemic bugs worth fixing

### 1. Slot-fill closer bugs (food-class label leaks into wrong pair)

The engine's "Strong; the X-on-Y call sits..." closer encodes a food-class slot (Y) that doesn't always match the actual food. Live examples from the sample:

- `Mount Gay Rum × Grilled Caesar` → *"the digestif-on-dessert pairing"* (Caesar is salad, Mount Gay isn't a digestif)
- `Shrimp Cocktail × Malibu Rum` → *"the light-spirit-on-fish-main pairing"* (cocktail is starter, not main)
- `Clam Chowder × Blood Oath Pact 10` → *"the digestif-on-dessert call"* (chowder is soup, BO Pact is bourbon)
- `Sauteed Garlic Spinach × Pikesville Rye` → *"slightly over-specified for the dessert"* (spinach is side)
- `Avion 44 × Rainbow Trout` → *"safe añejo-on-hearty-soup alongside"* (trout is fish-main)
- `Doctor Bird × Filet Mignon` → *"the light-spirit-on-dessert call"* (filet is steak)
- `Cristom × Mushrooms` → *"Peak Southern-French for the bird — preparation changes seasonally"* (mushrooms are not "the bird")
- `Hennessy Cognac × Broccoli Cheddar` → *"safe cognac-on-dessert alongside"*
- `El Mayor Reposado × Chilean Seabass` → *"accessible aged composed with dessert"*
- `Tomato Basil × Elijah Craig Private Barrel` → *"OGD 16 handles dessert density"* (tomato basil is soup)

Estimated population: 200–500 notes. Caused by closer templates generated from one (drinkClass × foodClass) bucket being substituted into pairs where the labels don't match.

### 2. LIGHT_SPIRIT class default applies tequila language to rums

Rums classified as LIGHT_SPIRIT inherit "blanco-tequila or light-rum register" / "agave-citrus thread" / "green-agave edge" phrasing — wrong for Mount Gay, Malibu, Bacardi, and other rums. The class needs subclasses (RUM_LIGHT vs TEQUILA_BLANCO).

- `Mount Gay Rum × Grilled Caesar` → *"green-agave-and-cane lift"* (Mount Gay has no agave)
- `Malibu Rum × Shrimp Cocktail` → *"blanco-tequila or light-rum register"*
- `Gran Patron Platinum × Butternut Squash` → *"blanco-tequila or light-rum register"* (Gran Patron is tequila — would be fine, but the same string appears for rums where "light-rum" is the only fit)

Same issue in HEAVY_SPIRIT for bottles like Doctor Bird Jamaica Rum and Myers's Rum, which inherit body text optimized for unspecified heavy spirits but get closers labeled "light-spirit-on-X".

### 3. Random content insertion (slot-fill leaks in body)

A handful of FxF/cross-pair notes contain words from neither side:

- `Chilean Seabass × Roasted Red Pepper Chickpea` → *"...the rye spice cuts the cheese richness"* (neither side has rye or cheese)

Estimated population: 20–60 notes. Worth scanning for `rye spice cuts the cheese` / similar misfits.

### 4. Wrong-bottle verdict-text leaks (still present)

The earlier verdict-leak fix caught notes where another bottle was the verdict subject. This one slipped through because the leaked verdict text isn't preceded by a `Strong;` / `Works;` tier label — it just appears mid-note:

- `Bone Marrow × Lagavulin 8` →
  > "* Lagavulin 8 -- Islay peat, younger-bottling, salt. The scotch backbone matches the rich marrow, and the smoke threads the starter. **Parker's Heritage brings HH's heritage collector-register craft to 26oz cap-fat — the annual-release provenance makes every pour a conversation about which edition you're serving;** Fernet Branca still cuts it harder."

Pattern: gold-tier legacy notes (`* {Bottle} --` opening) where the trailing "Parker's Heritage..." sentence is the leaked content. Worth a targeted scan.

### 5. Repeated FxF avoid body text (verbatim recycling)

Multiple food × big-red avoids share an identical body paragraph:

> *"this rich, creamy side's dairy-driven indulgence meets this structured red's cassis-and-dark-fruit frame, firm tannin, and oak-driven weight. The bold red's dark-fruit weight locks into the side's fat and umami; tannin cleans the dairy."*

Found in: Bone Marrow × Keenan Mernet, Au Gratin × Cyrus, Au Gratin × Le Volte, Creamed Spinach × Cade, Mushrooms × Corazón del Sol, Mushrooms × Venge Family Reserve, Au Gratin × Tenuta di Arceno, House Wedge × Heitz Trailside.

Comes from FxF templated body construction for `RICH_SIDE × ELEGANT_RED/BIG_RED`. Variant pool needed.

### 6. Under-promoted gold pairs (post-regen)

The earlier under-promotion fix appended `Gold standard;` closers, but a few gold pairs still read lower-tier or with no gold language:

- `Bacardi Rum × Seafood Tower` (gold) → ends with "Excellent; workhorse light rum on the seafood tower -- the kind of pour that earns a regular." — no Gold standard.
- `Joseph Mellot Sancerre × Market Fish` (gold) → ends with "Works — a capable side pour."

Small population; can be patched.

## Tier B — minor / stylistic

- **Possessive quirk:** `Booker's' cask-strength` and `Basil Hayden's' oak-and-vanilla` (drinkPossessive on names already ending in `'s` produces `'s'` — visually correct under English style but reads slightly off). Already partially fixed in engine; corpus has residual cases.
- **Adjective contradictions:** `Schloss Vollrads × Vegetable Curry` → "off-dry Riesling's stone-fruit sweetness" then "Crisp, vegetal wine" in same paragraph (sweet vs crisp).
- **Dish-fact assumptions:** `Filet Mignon × Cab Franc` → "the violet plays against escargot-butter" — escargot butter is an *optional* filet topping, not standard for every guest.
- **Competitor ref tonal mismatch:** `Larceny Small Batch × Prime Tartare` → ends with "; Fernet Branca still cuts it harder." — Fernet (bitter) doesn't actually "cut harder" than bourbon for tartare; sounds glib.

## Tier C — recurring closers worth varying further

The closer pool has expanded but a few phrases are still high-frequency in the sample:

- `the X-on-Y call lands without effort` (~80 hits in sample)
- `reliable across the meal` / `reliable headline pairing` (~60)
- `the call holds at full register without strain` (~40)
- `the X-on-Y call settles as a quiet partner` / `as a measured alongside` / `as a balanced companion` (~50 across these three)

Not bad per se — these are honest closers — but adding 5–10 more variants per tier would help.

## Recommendation order

1. **Fix the slot-fill closer bugs** (highest impact, fast). Trace where "X-on-Y" closers get their food-class label and ensure it matches the actual food.
2. **Subclass LIGHT_SPIRIT for rum vs tequila-blanco** so rum notes use rum language ("molasses cane", "Caribbean weight") and not "agave-citrus thread".
3. **Targeted scan for legacy gold leaks** — `* {Bottle} -- ... [unrelated bottle name]` pattern.
4. **FxF avoid body variants** for `RICH_SIDE × BIG_RED` / `RICH_SIDE × ELEGANT_RED`.
5. Other items as polish.
