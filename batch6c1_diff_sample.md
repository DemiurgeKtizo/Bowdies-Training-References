### STRONG (5 total)

  > St Supéry — Napa Cab, blackberry, cassis, mocha, full body, firm tannin. The firm tannin frames Porterhouse's dual-texture strip-and-filet and the cassis plays against both lean and fatty sides. Strong by-the-glass Cab for Porterhouse — accessible price for the dual-cut classic.

### WORKS (3 total)
- **House Wedge** _[44w]_
  > St Supéry — Napa Cab, blackberry, cassis, mocha, full body. The firm tannin cuts iceberg-and-bacon and the mocha plays against blue cheese dressing. Works as by-the-glass Cab for the wedge — unconventional red choice, though whites or rosés peak higher for the classic wedge.
- **Lardons** _[41w]_
  > St Supéry — Napa Cab, blackberry, cassis, mocha, full body. The firm tannin cuts lardons' bacon-fat richness and the mocha plays against the crisp pork. Works as by-the-glass Cab for lardons — Rutherford character frames the rich topper at accessible price.

### NEVER PAIR (4 total)
